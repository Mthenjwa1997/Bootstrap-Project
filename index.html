About Me

fullstack java Developer

Oyama Mthenjwa

Hello, my name is Oyama Mthenjwa. I am an entry-level Full-Stack Java Developer from Sasolburg, Free State, South Africa. I am passionate about software development and have actively pursued relevant skills and qualifications. I completed training programs with Devire, earning certifications in Agile Business Transformation and Full-Stack Java Development. Furthermore, I participated in a virtual global apprenticeship with Build3, a company based in India. This experience proved invaluable, as I had the opportunity to work on real-world projects and contribute to the company's development. During my apprenticeship, I gained hands-on experience building various applications, including those involving CRUD operations and GraphQL APIs. I am a highly motivated and eager learner, constantly seeking to expand my knowledge and skillset. I am confident that my dedication, combined with my technical abilities and willingness to learn, will allow me to adapt quickly to your team's environment and contribute meaningfully to your projects.
